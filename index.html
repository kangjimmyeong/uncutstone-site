Seven Puzzle

Seven pieces. One quiet cube.

Coming November 2026·iOS & Android

A calm 3D puzzle

Seven Puzzle is built around the soma cube: seven simple pieces that assemble into one perfect cube. Rotate each piece in your hand, find where it belongs, and set it in place — no timers, no lives, no pressure.

It is made for winding down: soft matte colours, a gentle pace, and a dark mode for late nights. Your progress is saved the moment a piece settles.

A quiet kind of puzzle

About the studio

Uncut Stone Studio is a small game studio in Incheon, South Korea. We make calm, tactile puzzle games — small worlds you can hold in one hand. Seven Puzzle is our first title.
