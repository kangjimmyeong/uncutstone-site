A calm 3D puzzle

Seven Puzzle is built around the soma cube: seven simple pieces that come together as one perfect cube. Turn each piece, find where it fits, and set it in place — no timers, no lives, no pressure.

It's made for winding down: soft matte colours, an easy pace, and dark mode for late nights. Your progress saves as soon as a piece settles.

A quiet kind of puzzle

About the studio

Uncut Stone Studio is a small game studio in Incheon, South Korea. We make calm, tactile puzzle games — small worlds you can hold in one hand. Seven Puzzle is our first title.
